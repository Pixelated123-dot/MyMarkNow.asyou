About Me

Hello! I'm Mark, a passionate high school student with a deep love for technology, robotics, and creative problem-solving. I enjoy building things that make life more efficient — whether it's a robot or a web application.

I believe that technology has the power to solve real-world problems and make life better for everyone. Whether it’s debugging a stubborn program or brainstorming ideas with a team, I enjoy every part of the creative process.

I’m constantly exploring new skills — from front-end development to working with microcontrollers — and I love challenging myself with projects that push my limits. My goal is not only to become a better coder but also a better collaborator and leader.

Outside of tech, I enjoy observing how things work, organizing my workflow to be as smooth as possible, and occasionally diving into the world of digital art and design. I’m someone who appreciates clear communication, innovative ideas, and the satisfaction of solving tough problems.
